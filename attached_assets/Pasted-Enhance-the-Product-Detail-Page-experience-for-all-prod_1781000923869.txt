Enhance the Product Detail Page experience for all product categories.

Applicable To:

* T-Shirts
* Mugs
* Caps
* Pens
* Badges
* Photo Frames
* Corporate Gifts

User Flow:

Products → Select Category → Select Product → Product Detail Page → Buy Now / Customize Now

Product Detail Page Requirements:

1. Product Gallery

* Display multiple high-quality product images.
* Show the product from different angles and sides.
* Allow image zoom on click.
* Allow users to switch between images easily.

Examples:

T-Shirts:

* Front View
* Back View
* Left Side View
* Right Side View
* Close-Up Fabric View

Mugs:

* Front View
* Back View
* Side View
* Handle Side View

Caps:

* Front View
* Back View
* Side View
* Top View

Apply the same multi-angle image system to all products.

2. Product Overview

Display a short and easy-to-read summary before any purchase action.

Include:

* Product Name
* Product Category
* Product Price
* Product Description
* Available Colors
* Available Sizes (if applicable)
* Material Information
* Printing Type
* Estimated Delivery Time

3. Product Specifications

Show specifications in a clean format.

Examples:

For T-Shirts:

* Fabric Material
* GSM
* Fit Type
* Sleeve Type
* Available Sizes

For Mugs:

* Material
* Capacity
* Finish Type

For Caps:

* Material
* Adjustable Strap
* Print Area

4. Purchase Actions

After viewing the details, users can choose:

* Buy Now
* Customize Now
* Add to Cart

5. Related Products

Display similar products from the same category below the product details.

Important:

* Do not directly open Buy Now or Customize pages when a product is clicked.
* Always open a dedicated Product Detail Page first.
* Show multiple product images from different angles so customers can clearly understand the actual appearance and quality of the product.
* Apply the same structure consistently across all product categories.
* Keep the layout clean, professional, mobile-friendly, and easy to navigate.
