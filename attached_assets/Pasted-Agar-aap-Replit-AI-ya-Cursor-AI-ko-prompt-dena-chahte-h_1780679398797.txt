> * Maintain the current branding and simply enhance the first impression of the homepage.
>

>
> 1. Page loads with hero background visible.
> 2. Logo fades in and scales up smoothly.
> 3. Logo performs a gentle floating animation.
> 4. Hero title and subtitle fade upward one after another.
> 5. CTA button ("Order Now") appears with a subtle fade and hover lift effect.
>
> ### Styling:
>
> * Premium, modern, and clean.
> * Smooth transitions (ease-in-out).
> * No flashy effects, glassmorphism, or unnecessary redesign.
> * Keep loading experience elegant and professional like a high-end printing brand website.
>
> Use Framer Motion (if React) or pure CSS animations for optimal performance.
