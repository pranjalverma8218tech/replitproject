Redesign the entire website navigation bar to look like a premium ecommerce and custom printing brand.

Current Problem:

The navbar looks basic, small, and difficult to scan from a distance.

Menu items are not visually prominent.
Categories are not highlighted.
The navbar lacks a premium branded appearance.

=================================
NAVBAR GOAL
===========

Create a modern premium ecommerce navigation similar to top brands.

Examples:

* Nike
* Adidas
* Vistaprint
* Printrove
* Printify

The navbar must feel professional, premium, and trustworthy.

=================================
NAVBAR HEIGHT
=============

Increase navbar height.

Current navbar feels compressed.

Use:

* More vertical padding
* Better spacing
* Larger click areas

LOGO AREA

Make logo section more prominent.

Requirements:

* Larger logo

* Strong brand visibility

Home
Products
Customize

Contact

* Better letter spacing

HOME
